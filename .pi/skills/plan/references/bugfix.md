## Bugfix Planning Lens

Use this reference when planning corrective work for broken behavior.

Check for:

- the observed failure and how it is reproduced
- the expected behavior versus the broken behavior
- root-cause confidence and any remaining uncertainty
- regression prevention through tests, assertions, or instrumentation
- whether adjacent cleanup or guardrails belong in scope or should be split out

Keep the plan anchored to the actual bug. Avoid turning a focused fix into an opportunistic rewrite.
